When I began my S P A C E Rising Residency in September, I was struck by how I was given the opportunity to dance in a studio for hours. For the first five weeks, I spent my time dancing and exploring my connection to self, to dance, to the space. However, I felt my movement was empty and the show date seemed so far away.
